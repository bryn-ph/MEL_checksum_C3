Balance Board

Navigate life events. Keep your wellbeing balanced.

Theme: Mental Health & Wellbeing — Australia

How to play

  • Roll the dice to move along the board (12 tiles).
  • Each tile may trigger an event card. Cards affect your Wellbeing (0 - 100).
  • If your Wellbeing reaches 0, game over. Reach tile 12 with ≥50 Wellbeing to win.
  • Use Support cards to recover. Choices matter.
