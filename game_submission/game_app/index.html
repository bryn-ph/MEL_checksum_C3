House of Cards

A card game about the housing crisis — buy, rent, survive.

Front-end only — HTML, CSS, JS

How to play

  1. Choose number of players and rounds, then Start Game.
  2. Each player starts with cash and a small loan capacity. On your turn you draw 1 card.
  3. Play up to 2 cards from your hand (property, action, or event responses).
  4. At end of each round (after all players), rent is collected and every 3 rounds a Crisis card affects market.
  5. After set rounds, the player with highest Net Worth (cash + properties - debt) wins.
